About Julian Shively

Julian Shively is a singer, songwriter, and producer known for intimate, story-driven music that blends jazz, pop, and contemporary sounds. His work explores personal narratives and emotional landscapes, creating experiences that resonate deeply with audiences.

Based in the Pacific Northwest, Julian performs regularly in chamber series, jazz clubs, and private events, crafting performances that are both engaging and immersive. His music is available on streaming platforms, with new releases and collaborations continually expanding his portfolio.
