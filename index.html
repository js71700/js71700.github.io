About Julian

Julian Shively is a versatile musician known for his proficiency on both the piano and cello, as well as his original compositions and arrangements.

Based in Montreal, Julian frequently collaborates with jazz and classical ensembles, bringing a unique blend of technical mastery and emotional depth to every performance.
